## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID. To manage or cancel:

1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone or iPad
2. Tap your name → **Subscriptions**
3. Select **Bankrolly Pro** and choose your plan or tap **Cancel Subscription**

You can also open **Settings → Manage subscription** inside the app.

### I purchased Pro but features are still locked

Open the paywall in the app and tap **Restore purchases**. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID that made the purchase. If it still doesn't unlock, email us and we'll fix it.

### How do I delete my account?

Open **Settings** in the app and tap **Delete account**. This permanently removes your account and all associated data. You can also email us to request deletion.

### How do I sign in?

Bankrolly supports Sign in with Apple, Google, or email and password. If you have trouble signing in, try again in a few minutes or contact us.

### Is Bankrolly a gambling app?

No. Bankrolly is a bankroll and session **tracking** tool for informational and analytical purposes only. It does not provide gambling services or facilitate real-money betting.
