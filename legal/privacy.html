Privacy Policy — Bankrolly

Last updated: 12 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Bankrolly ("we", "us", or "the app") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data we collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication identifiers via our authentication provider (Clerk Inc.). If you sign in via Apple, Google, GitHub or Discord, we receive only the email address and a user identifier from those providers.

Subscription status: If you purchase a subscription, Apple processes the payment. We receive your subscription status (active, expired, in trial) via Apple's StoreKit and our subscription management provider (RevenueCat Inc.). We never see your payment details — those are handled solely by Apple.

Poker tracking data: Cash sessions, tournaments, hand notes, player notes, trips and expenses you log are stored locally on your device in an SQLite database. We do not transmit, collect, or have access to this data.

2. Data we do NOT collect

  • Your real name (unless you enter it yourself)
  • Your location
  • Your contacts, photos, or device identifiers for advertising
  • Analytics or usage telemetry that identifies you
  • Any of your poker session, hand, player or trip data

3. How we use your information

The email address and authentication identifier are used solely to:

  • Authenticate you on app launch
  • Scope your Player Notes and Trips to your account (so different users on the same device see only their own data)
  • Sync your subscription entitlement across your devices

4. Third-party services

We use the following providers to operate the app. Each handles a narrow piece of functionality:

  • Clerk Inc. — authentication. See clerk.com/privacy.
  • RevenueCat Inc. — subscription state. See revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • Apple Inc. — payment processing for subscriptions. Handled by your Apple ID.
  • Expo (EAS) — over-the-air update delivery for app code (no user data).

5. Data retention

Your authentication record persists until you delete your account by contacting us. Your local poker data persists on your device until you uninstall the app or use the "Reset database" function in Settings.

6. Your rights

You can request deletion of your account and any associated authentication record by emailing us at the address below. Local data on your device is fully under your control via the app's Reset function.

7. Children

Bankrolly is not directed at children under 17. Poker is an adult activity; we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the date above and post the new version at this URL.
